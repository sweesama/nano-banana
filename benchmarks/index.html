Methodology & limits

Each benchmark includes original inputs, masks (if used), and fixed parameters (seed, steps, guidance, strength/scheduler when relevant) for apples‑to‑apples comparisons. Hardware, model version, and randomness can influence results; we therefore also publish failure cases and notes around tricky regions (hands/fingers, tiny accessories).
