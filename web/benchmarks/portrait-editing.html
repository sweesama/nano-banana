Methodology

We provide original inputs, any masks used, and keep seed/steps/guidance constant across models. Strength/scheduler are disclosed. Exports use identical resolution/format for fair visual inspection.

  • Environment variance (hardware, CUDA/cuDNN, model minor versions) can affect fine details.
  • Tricky regions: hands/fingers, tiny accessories, text, thin edges. We include failure cases when they occur.
  • We avoid cherry‑picking: first acceptable output within the fixed step budget is published.
  • Comparators may include FLUX Kontext and others when relevant.
