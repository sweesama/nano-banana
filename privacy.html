Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This privacy policy describes how Nano Banana ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • Page views and session duration
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral sources

Technical Data

Our web server automatically collects:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser user agents
  • Access timestamps
  • Requested URLs

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand content preferences and usage patterns
  • Monitor for security issues and abuse
  • Generate aggregate statistics for internal analysis

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information for research or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses:

  • Google Analytics - Web analytics (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Vercel - Website hosting (subject to Vercel's privacy policy)

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Security and fraud prevention

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website or the information provided in our About page.
